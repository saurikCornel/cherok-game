Rules of the Game "Cherok Online"

Welcome to "Cherok Online," a modern twist on the classic game of Chapaev Checkers! Strategize, outmaneuver your opponents, and claim victory in this exciting battle of wits and skill. Are you ready to dominate the board?

How to Play

The game is based on the traditional rules of Chapaev Checkers, with a few modern enhancements. Here’s how it works:

  • Objective: The goal is to eliminate all of your opponent's checkers from the board or block them so they cannot move.
  • Movement: Players take turns moving their checkers diagonally forward. Regular checkers can only move one square at a time.
  • Capturing: If an opponent's checker is in a diagonal square adjacent to yours and the square immediately beyond it is empty, you can "jump" over the opponent's checker to capture it. Multiple jumps in a single turn are allowed if possible.
  • Kings: When a checker reaches the farthest row (the "king row"), it is crowned a king. Kings can move both forward and backward diagonally, giving them greater mobility.
  • Winning: The game ends when one player captures all of the opponent's checkers or blocks them so they cannot make a move.

Game Modes

  • Player vs Player: Challenge a friend or another online player in a head-to-head match.
  • Player vs Bot: Test your skills against an AI opponent with adjustable difficulty levels.
